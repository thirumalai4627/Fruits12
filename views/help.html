Frequently Asked Questions

To place an order, simply browse through our fresh fruits and vegetables, select the items you want, and click on the "Add to Cart" button. Then proceed to the checkout to complete your order.

We accept multiple payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI payments. All payments are securely processed.

After placing an order, you will receive an email with the tracking number and order status. You can track your order directly from the email or through the "Track Order" section on our website.
